Why this book is different

1

No "Anda" Allowed

Native speakers rarely use the formal "Anda". We teach you to use Titles (Mas, Mba, Pak) so you don't sound like a robot.

2

Zero Copula

Stop worrying about "am, is, are". Indonesian doesn't have them! We teach you the shortcut to fluency.

3

Survival Focused

Learn only the 20% of vocabulary that gives you 80% of the results. Directions, Food, Money, and Socializing.
